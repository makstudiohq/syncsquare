13 · FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is our data shared with any other firm?

No. Your SyncSquare is private and 1-of-1, built only for your firm. Another customer never shares its memory.

Is this a CRM?

No. A CRM only knows what somebody remembers to type into it. SyncSquare learns from the messages, calls, notes and files your team already creates.

Can it do things, or does it only answer questions?

Both. Ask about a relationship, or tell it to prepare the memo, meeting notes or follow-up. The work is built from the firm's real history, with the sources attached.

Does it work for firms larger than 50 people?

Yes. The same private brain can connect a five-person boutique or a firm with 100+ people and several offices.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude?

A general AI chat starts from zero and only knows the context you paste in. SyncSquare is a permanent, private memory built from your firm's own history. It stays current every day and keeps compounding.
